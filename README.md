# technical_trainings
A collection of hands-on technical trainings, course exercises, and notes completed across topics in accelerated computing, robotics, machine learning, 
and related engineering tools and personal intrests. 

## Structure
Current layout:

cuda-cpp-intro/ — CUDA C/C++ notebook, README, and completion screen shot

Planned additions may include additional NVIDIA training folders for Isaac Sim, Isaac Lab, RAPIDS, and robotics workflows, as well as others such as AWS or Azure.

## Purpose
This repo is meant to document applied learning through code, notebooks, short writeups, and small experiments rather than just listing completed courses.
The goal is to keep training outputs interpretable, reproducible, and easy to reference from a resume, LinkedIn, or project portfolio.

### Notes
*Most folders are lightweight by design and may contain notebooks, markdown summaries, screenshots, or source files depending on the training.
As the repository grows, each subfolder will serve as a self-contained record of what was studied, implemented, and learned.*
